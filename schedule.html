Phase 1 · Posture & Setup
From Week 3 onward

Weekly schedule template

Three days, plus a bonus fourth. Each day has a single purpose. Don’t try to do everything in every session — that’s how range time gets wasted.

Day 1 — Range Block (60–90 min)

Where: outdoor range. Goal: technical work, mostly blocked, on the current phase’s primary drill.

TimeBlockWhat you do
0–10Mobility10-minute mobility routine. Don’t skip — direct fix for the cause of the miss pattern.
10–20Slow rehearsals20 reps, no ball: wall drill or current phase’s primary drill at 50% speed.
20–55Drill blockPhase-appropriate drill set. 7-iron in Phase 1; mix in 5-iron / hybrid / driver in later phases.
55–75Integration20 balls. Full swings at 80% effort. Rotate clubs (8i, 6i, hybrid). Keep the drill feel without using the prop.
75–90Cool-down10 short-game shots. Two repeats of mobility hip-flexor stretch.

Day 2 — Launch Monitor (60–90 min)

Where: launch monitor bay. Goal: measure, don’t just hit. Start each session by writing down your top 2 KPIs for the day, and review the numbers between sets, not after.

TimeBlockWhat you do
0–10Mobility + warmupMobility routine, then 10 swings 9i with no measurement — just settle in.
10–20Baseline set10 shots 7-iron, full speed. Record: smash factor, club path, face angle, face-to-path, low-point, dispersion.
20–55Targeted workIf face-to-path > +3°: lead-wrist flexion + split-grip set. If low-point varies > 2°: wall drill set. If dispersion > 12 yds: Pump Drill set. 5 drill reps → 5 measured shots → repeat.
55–80Driver workPhase 2+. 10 driver shots, alternating ‘shaped’ (intentional draw start) with ‘straight.’ Track face-to-path and dispersion.
80–90Closer set5 tournament shots with full pre-shot routine (Phase 3+). Log committed-and-executed count.

Day 3 — Course or Random (90 min, or 9 holes)

Where: golf course if possible, otherwise random practice on the range. Goal: transfer.

  • If on the course: play 9 holes solo, drop a second ball on tee shots and approaches inside 200 yards. Track fairways hit, GIR, scrambling, and miss tendency by hole. Ignore score.
  • If on the range: Random 9-Shot Game — 3 different clubs × 3 different targets, full pre-shot routine on each, never two in a row from the same setup. Score yourself 1 point per shot that lands on the intended side of the target.

Bonus Day — Short Game + Body (45–60 min)

Where: short-game area, practice green, backyard. Goal: maintain a strength, recover the body, end the week tuned-up. Intentionally not another full-swing day.

  • 15 min: variety chipping. 3 lies × 3 clubs (52°, PW, 8i bump-and-run) × 3 targets. 27 shots total.
  • 15 min: 3-6-9 putting drill. Make 3 in a row from 3', then 6', then 9'. Restart at 3' on any miss.
  • 15 min: backyard wedge tempo work. Low-and-slow rehearsals; no scoring.
  • 15 min: full mobility routine + foam roll thoracic spine and lats.
