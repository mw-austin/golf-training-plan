Quick reference

Print this page or screenshot it. Stick it in the bag.

This week’s job

PhaseOne-line job
1 (W 1–2)Glutes against the wall. Trail glute touches on backswing, lead glute replaces it on downswing. NO DRIVER.
2 (W 3–4)Motorcycle the lead wrist from the top. Bowed wrist at impact. Driver back, only on monitor day.
3 (W 5–6)Pre-shot routine on every full swing. Random 9-Shot Game once a week. Never the same setup twice in a row.
4 (W 7–8)Play golf. Routine on every shot. No new thoughts.

Pre-shot routine (every full-swing shot)

Think Box (behind ball, 5–10s)Play Box (over ball, 3–4s)
1. Read the lie, wind, distance, trouble.
2. Pick a specific target.
3. Pick shape, trajectory, club.
4. Commit out loud: ‘[club], [shape], [target].’		 1. Step in, align to target.
2. One look at the target.
3. One swing thought (the phase feel).
4. Trigger → swing.

Top-3 launch monitor KPIs to watch on Day 2

  • Driver face-to-path — target ±2°. Above +3° = work the Motorcycle + Split-Grip set.
  • 7-iron AoA — target −5° to −3°. Variability or positive = work the Wall Drill set.
  • 7-iron smash factor — target ≥ 1.30. Below = strike location problem; check impact tape.

Three rules every session

  • Slow before fast. Every drill at 50% before any speed.
  • One feel at a time. More than one = zero.
  • End on a good shot. Walk away after 3 bad reps.

If something’s off

  • Slice back? → Wall Drill + Motorcycle. No driver next session.
  • Fat/thin returning? → Step-Through Drill, ball back one ball-width.
  • Bad round? → Rest, then Bonus Day. Do not chase it.
