Per-session and per-round logging

Tracking: what to log each week

If you don’t measure it, you can’t tell if the plan is working. Two log shapes — one for sessions, one for rounds.

Per-session log (after every Day 1 / Day 2 session)

5 lines, 1 minute. Use the KPI tracker for the launch monitor numbers; capture the rest in your notes app.

Pre-shot routine adherenceOut of full-swing shots, how many got the full routine? (Goal: 100% by Phase 4.)

What ‘success’ looks like at week 8

  • Penalty tee shots: 1 or fewer per round (from your current 1–2 per round).
  • Duffed/thinned long shots: 1 or fewer per round.
  • Fairways hit: +2 per round vs. baseline.
  • Average score: 86–90 (a solid mid-80s pattern, with the occasional 84 or 91).
  • Comfort over the ball: subjective 1–10 scale, target 7+ (from current 4–5).
  • Launch monitor 7-iron face-to-path average: ±2°.
