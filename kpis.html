Your gap to mid-handicap

To move from the 16–22 bracket into the 10–15 bracket, the data says you need to:

  • Add 5–10 mph driver ball speed (currently 128–135 → target 135–145). Comes mostly from the early-extension fix and a slightly upward AoA.
  • Shrink driver dispersion from ~35–50 yds wide to ~25–35 yds. The face-to-path fix.
  • Improve GIR from 18–25% to 33–37%. Iron quality and approach distance control — both downstream of the same swing fixes.
  • Knock off 1–2 putts per round and 1 three-putt per round. Mostly speed-control work.

Strokes-gained back-of-the-envelope: those four shifts add up to roughly 4–6 strokes — the move from a mid-90s scoring average into the 86–88 range.
